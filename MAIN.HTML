About BIRS

Empowering Student Innovation

BUAN Innovations and Research Society (BIRS) is dedicated to connecting students with experts, organizations, and companies that can help them upskill and transform their ideas into tangible businesses.

We believe that innovation happens at the intersection of diverse disciplines, ideas, and perspectives. Through workshops, networking events, mentorship programs, and collaborative projects, we create opportunities for students to explore their potential and make a meaningful impact.
